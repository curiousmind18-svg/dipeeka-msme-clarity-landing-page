# MSME Marketing Clarity Checklist Landing Page

Static landing page for Dipeeka Saboo’s lead magnet: the MSME Marketing Clarity Checklist.

## Strategy summary

- Page goal: generate checklist requests from MSME and founder-led business owners whose marketing feels scattered.
- Target visitor: MSME founders, service business owners, coaches, consultants, high-ticket service providers, women entrepreneurs, and founder-led businesses in India, UAE, Africa, USA, and similar growth markets.
- Core promise: before spending more on marketing, identify whether the growth system behind the marketing is clear enough to convert.
- Primary offer: MSME Marketing Clarity Checklist.
- Primary CTA: “Get the checklist” / “Request the checklist”.
- CTA destination: currently uses `mailto:dipeeka@cmcpros.in` with the subject “CLARITY Checklist Request”. Replace with the final form, CRM, WhatsApp, or automation link when ready.
- Proof used: Dipeeka’s positioning, agency execution background, performance marketing, landing page development, AI systems, customer journey thinking, podcast host identity, and Curiousmind Consulting ecosystem.
- Proof intentionally not used: fake client logos, fake revenue numbers, fake testimonials, fake scarcity, or invented guarantees.
